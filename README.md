# 🌊 VARUNA OCEANIX
### Integrated Platform for Crowdsourced Ocean Hazard Reporting & Social Media Analytics

🚀 **SIH 2025 | Ministry of Earth Sciences (INCOIS)**

---

## 📌 Overview
India’s **7,500 km coastline** is prone to multiple oceanic and coastal hazards such as **tsunamis, storm surges, high waves, and oil spills**.  

While **INCOIS** provides satellite-based hazard alerts, **real-time community inputs remain limited**.

**VARUNA OCEANIX** bridges this gap through an **AI-driven multi-source platform** that combines data from **citizens, fishermen, and social media** into a unified **Decision Support System (DSS)**.

---

## 💡 Problem Statement

**PS ID:** SIH25039  
**Theme:** Disaster Management  
**Ministry:** Ministry of Earth Sciences (INCOIS)

**Problem:**  
Develop an **Integrated Platform for Crowdsourced Ocean Hazard Reporting & Social Media Analytics**.

---

## 🧠 Proposed Solution

An **Integrated Mobile + Web Platform** that collects, analyzes, and visualizes **multi-source hazard data** to assist authorities, coastal communities, and INCOIS in **faster and data-driven decision making**.

---

## 🔑 Key Modules

### 📱 Citizen App
- Geotagged **text, photo, and video** hazard reports
- **Offline support** for low-network coastal regions

### 🤖 AI-driven Messaging & Social Media Module
- **WhatsApp Bot** for fishermen alerts, voice notes, and images
- **NLP Engine** scans:
  - WhatsApp
  - Twitter
  - Facebook
  - YouTube  

Detects **hazard-related trends, keywords, and sentiment analysis**

### 🌊 Marine & Economic Module
- Reports **oil slicks, fish kills, and marine hazards**
- Estimates **economic loss**
- Provides **ecological impact analysis**

### 🖥️ Dashboard (Decision Support System)
- **Real-time interactive hazard map**
- Visualizes **citizen + fishermen + social media data**
- **Role-based access**
- **INCOIS API integration**
