# 🌊 VARUNA OCEANIX

### Integrated Platform for Crowdsourced Ocean Hazard Reporting & Social Media Analytics  
**SIH 2025 | Ministry of Earth Sciences (INCOIS)**

---

## 🚀 Overview

India’s 7,500 km coastline is prone to multiple oceanic and coastal hazards such as tsunamis, storm surges, high waves, and oil spills. While INCOIS provides satellite-based hazard alerts, real-time community inputs remain limited.  
**VARUNA OCEANIX** bridges this gap through an AI-driven, multi-source platform combining **citizen, fishermen, and social media data** into one intelligent **Decision Support System (DSS)**.

## 🧠 Proposed Solution

An integrated **Mobile + Web Platform** that collects, analyzes, and visualizes multi-source hazard data to assist authorities, communities, and INCOIS in faster, data-backed decision-making.

### 🔑 Key Modules

- **Citizen App:**  
  - Geotagged text, photo, and video reports.  
  - Works offline for low-network coastal regions.  

- **AI-driven Messaging & Social Media Module:**  
  - WhatsApp Bot to capture fishermen alerts, voice notes, and images.  
  - NLP engine scans WhatsApp, Twitter, Facebook, and YouTube for hazard-related trends, sentiment, and keywords.  

- **Marine & Economic Module:**  
  - Reports oil slicks, fish kills, and economic loss estimates.  
  - Provides ecological impact analysis.  

- **Dashboard (DSS):**  
  - Real-time map-based visualization of citizen, fishermen, and social data.  
  - Role-based access, filters, and INCOIS API integration.

## ✨ Key Features

- 🛰️ **Multi-source hazard input** (Citizen + Fishermen + Social Media)
